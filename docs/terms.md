# Beer Snap Terms & Posting Guidelines (Draft)

## Public by Default
All Beer Snap uploads are public. There is no private posting in v1.

## What to Post
- Only photos of beer.
- Keep it safe and respectful.
- No abusive, hateful, or explicit content.

## Moderation
- Reports and community dislikes help flag content.
- We may remove posts that violate these rules.

## Safety
- Do not share personal or sensitive information in images.

## Updates
These guidelines may evolve as the community grows.
